About

Hello~ my name is Luz Irene L. Cortez from the beautiful city of Surigao, Philippines. I graduated 2-years ago with a Bachelor's degree in Ceramics Engineering. After graduating, I applied to roles related to my field of study, but couldn't land one. A year became two, still, I don't have a job. The airports of different fields of study are always full, and am still above the clouds trying to find, even just an open space to land on. After two and a half years of roaming above the often gloomy clouds, I finally found an open space! It's a road! But, the road signs says Front-End Development, Back-End Development, and Full-Stack Development. "Where in the world am I?". Four months later, I am now working on building my place from ground up in this city called Web Development.
